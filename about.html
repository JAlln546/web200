Aimee Serene!

My name is Aimee Serene. My Mother gave me that first and middle name, and throughout my life as a free spirited wildchild we always giggled about how the last thing anyone would call me was “Serene”.

I was loud, both in my physical presence and vocal display (and still am today, go figure!). What has changed though is my ability to remain grounded. I found this through dance, sound and vibrational healing modalities.

The greatest lesson I have learned, is that every step I’ve taken in my life has brought me here to where I am today. Granted me the opportunity to learn more, expand my horizons and bring my gift into the world to share.

I wouldn’t be here without having been there, so allow to tell you where I’ve been and what I’ve been learning along the way! Throughout the past 20 years, I have studied and practiced in the following areas (these leave out the decades of “work” I’ve done in the areas that do not directly apply to this area of study):

  • Massage Therapy, Nutrition and Healing with Whole Foods (Heartwood Institute 2007)
  • Yoga (personal practice for 16 years)
  • Pilates (Instructor 2000)
  • Dancer (18 years) & Dance Instructor (ages 4-11)
  • Sound Healer (beginning in 2017 - infinity)

My goal is to touch 100,000 hearts and minds with the this work. While payment for treatments is appreciated, the biggest reason I am doing this is to focus on dedicating my time to those members of our community who many not otherwise have an opportunity to experience such a decadent and relaxing form of healing and meditation.

I find it best to get to know one another with stories of our experiences in life that brought us to where we are today. Though it is long, I welcome you to learn as much as you would like to about me below in my story of healing and discovery.

If I say anything that rings true for you please feel free to contact me, we are all on our life’s journey, and I would love to hear about yours too!
